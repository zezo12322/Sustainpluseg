Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an EIA study take?

Timelines vary by project complexity. A Category A EIA typically takes 3–6 months for fieldwork, analysis, and report preparation. Category B studies can be completed in 6–12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines at project kickoff.

Do you work outside Egypt?

Yes. We have delivered projects in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Libya, Sudan, Ethiopia, and several Sub-Saharan African countries. We partner with local consultants where required by local regulations.

Can you help with an existing permit violation?

Absolutely. We assist clients in conducting gap analyses, developing corrective action plans, and engaging with regulators to resolve compliance issues and avoid or minimize penalties.

Do you provide training in Arabic?

Yes. All our training programs are available in both Arabic and English. We can also deliver bilingual sessions for teams with mixed language backgrounds.
