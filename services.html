Permits & Approvals

Expert navigation of Egypt's complex environmental and operational regulatory landscape — from application to final permit.

Securing environmental permits in Egypt requires deep knowledge of Law 4/1994, its amendments, and the procedural requirements of EEAA, local Environmental Affairs Offices, and sector-specific regulators. Delays in permitting can stall projects and increase costs significantly.

Sustain Plus provides end-to-end permit management — preparing technically sound applications, coordinating with regulatory authorities, responding to queries, and tracking approvals — so your project stays on schedule.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

Rigorous, regulator-accepted EIA studies that enable informed decision-making and project approval.

EIA is a cornerstone of Egypt's environmental regulatory framework under Law 4/1994. Sustain Plus has prepared over 80 EIA studies accepted by EEAA, covering projects in energy, manufacturing, transportation, real estate, and agro-industry.

Our EIA studies are thorough, well-structured, and written to meet both Egyptian regulatory requirements and international standards (IFC, World Bank, Equator Principles), making them suitable for projects seeking international financing.

  • Scoping and Terms of Reference preparation
  • Environmental baseline surveys
  • Impact prediction and significance evaluation
  • Mitigation and monitoring plans
  • Public participation and stakeholder engagement
  • EEAA review support and follow-up
