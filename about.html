Who We Are

Sustain Plus is a premier Egyptian environmental and engineering consultancy established to bridge the gap between economic progress and environmental stewardship. We serve developers, government entities, financial institutions, and NGOs across Egypt and the broader MENA and African regions.

Our multidisciplinary team — comprising environmental scientists, civil engineers, legal specialists, and community development experts — delivers integrated solutions grounded in international standards and local regulatory knowledge.

  • Accredited by the Egyptian Environmental Affairs Agency (EEAA)
  • Certified to IFC Performance Standards and World Bank safeguard policies
  • Member of the Egyptian Syndicate of Engineers
  • ISO 14001 Environmental Management System experience

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide scientifically rigorous, practically effective, and ethically grounded environmental consulting services that empower our clients to achieve their goals while safeguarding natural resources for future generations.

Our Vision

To be the foremost environmental consultancy in the MENA region — recognized for technical excellence, innovation, and measurable positive impact on communities and ecosystems.

Our Commitment

We commit to transparent communication, continuous improvement, and long-term partnerships that extend beyond project delivery — supporting clients throughout the environmental compliance lifecycle.
